UBC Business Analytics Open Learning Resource

Analytics@Sauder is currently a University of British Columbia (UBC) Teaching and Learning Enhancement Fund (TLEF) sponsored project. The objective of this project is to curate a collection of open-source business analytics tools and examples, foster a business analytics online learning community, and facilitate conversations around data analytics within a broader business context. We hope that this project can be helpful to business analytics professionals of all skill levels; at the same time, we welcome suggestions, contributions, and collaborations of all kind.

Navigate This Website

The Blog page (UBC Business Analytics Blog) is a platform where users can share posts and have discussions around any news, tools, and applications involving data analytics in the business world. The Projects page contains a list of Jupyter Notebooks with applications of analytics in various business domains. Detailed information on how to contribute as well as tutorials for Git, GitHub, Jupyter Notebook, Syzygy, and Binder can all be found on the Help page.
